The job placement center at your school surveys all graduating seniors at the school.
Their report about the survey provides numerical summaries such as the average 
starting salary and the percentage of students earning more than $30,000 a year.

a. Are these statistical analyses descriptive or inferential? Explain.
b. Are these numerical summaries better characterized as
statistics or as parameters?
-------------

a. These are descriptive statistics, because they summarize the actual data collected from all graduating seniors. 
No predictions or generalizations are being made beyond the group surveyed.

b. These numerical summaries are parameters, since the survey included all graduating seniors, not just a sample. 
They describe values from the entire population.

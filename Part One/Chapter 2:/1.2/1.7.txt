1.7 Ebook use During the spring semester in 2014, an ebook
survey was administered to students at Winthrop University.
Of the 170 students sampled, 45% indicated that they had
used ebooks for their academic work. 
Identify: 
(a) the sample, 
(b) the population, 
(c) the sample statistic reported.
------------

a. A sample is the subset of the population for whom we have data.
Here, the 170 students surveyed make up the sample.

b. The population is the total group we want to draw conclusions about.
In this case, the population is all students at Winthrop University.

c. The sample statistic is a numerical summary of the sample.
Here, the 45% who reported using ebooks is the sample statistic.

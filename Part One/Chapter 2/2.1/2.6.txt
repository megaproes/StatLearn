2.6 Discrete or continuous? Identify each of the following variables as continuous or discrete.
a. The upload speed of an Internet connection
b. The number of apps installed on a tablet
c. The height of a tree
d. The number of emails you send in a day
----------

a. Continuous - Upload speed can take on any value within a range and is often measured with decimals.
b. Discrete — The number of apps is a countable value and must be a whole number.
c. Continuous — Tree height is measurable and can include fractional values.
d. Discrete — Number of emails is a whole-number count, so it's a discrete variable.
